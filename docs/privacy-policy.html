Privacy Policy for SubTerrania

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Overview

SubTerrania is a single-player, offline turn-based strategy game developed by Atlyn. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains our practices regarding data collection.

Data Collection

SubTerrania does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • The app does not require an internet connection
  • The app does not collect personal information
  • The app does not use analytics or tracking services
  • The app does not use advertising SDKs
  • The app does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or files

Permissions

The app requests only the VIBRATE permission, which is used for haptic feedback during gameplay. This permission does not involve any data collection.

Third-Party Services

SubTerrania does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics providers, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

While SubTerrania does not target children under 13, the app is safe for users of all ages as it does not collect any data.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
